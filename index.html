Get to know me!

Hello! My name is Sally, I am 24 years old and I am what you can call a Software Developer in the making.
You know what though, my education up until I took up the Code Fellows Advanced Software Development course was mainly in the business field; I studies business in high school during my O-levels and A-levels and continued to get a Bachelor's Degree in Marketing after graduating high school. I even worked in the field for close to four years; my last position was an Operations Manager in an apparels company.
After Leaving my last job, I noticed this great pull in the market towards technical skills, especially with the rise of the COVID-19 pandemic. That is when I realized that in order for me to be able to fulfil my goals in life, I need to adjust to the demands of the market. That my friend, is how a Marketing graduate with no previous programming skills has now made the website you are visiting.

Just remember, it is never the wrong time to do what is best for you!
